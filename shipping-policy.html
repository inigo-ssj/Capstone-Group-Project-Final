Shipping Policy

At Everwear, we strive to ensure that your shopping experience is smooth and hassle-free. Below are the details of our shipping policy to help you understand our process.

1. Shipping Locations

We currently ship to the following regions:

  • Domestic: We deliver to all provinces, including Metro Manila, Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.
  • International: Selected countries listed during the checkout process.

If your location is not listed, please contact us at support@everwear.com.

2. Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

3. Shipping Options and Delivery Times

We offer the following shipping options during checkout:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days.
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days.
  • International Shipping: 7-14 business days.

Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on location, weather conditions, and carrier performance.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the weight, size of your order, and the delivery location. Free shipping is available for orders above [Amount, e.g., $50].

5. Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with tracking details. Use the provided link to monitor your shipment status.

6. Lost or Damaged Shipments

If your order is lost or arrives damaged, please contact us immediately at support@everwear.com. We will investigate and assist you in resolving the issue.

7. Questions?

If you have any questions or concerns about our Shipping Policy, feel free to reach out to us at support@everwear.com.
