Frequently Asked Questions

What is the shipping policy for Everwear?

We offer standard and express shipping to all locations in the Philippines. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days, and delivery typically takes between 3-7 business days depending on your location.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the progress of your delivery through the courier’s tracking system.

Can I change my order after it's been placed?

We process orders quickly, but if you wish to make any changes, please contact our support team as soon as possible. We may be able to make changes before the order is shipped, but unfortunately, we cannot guarantee changes once your order is in processing or shipped.

How do I return an item?

We accept returns on most items within 14 days of delivery, provided the product is unused and in its original packaging. Please visit our Return & Refund Policy for more detailed instructions on how to initiate a return.

Are all items eligible for return or exchange?

Unfortunately, certain items such as accessories or undergarments are non-returnable due to hygiene reasons. Please refer to our Return & Refund Policy for a full list of non-returnable items.

How can I pay for my order?

We accept payments via credit and debit cards, PayPal, and COD (Cash on Delivery) for orders within the Philippines. You can choose your preferred payment method during checkout.

Do you offer gift cards?

Currently, we do not offer gift cards. However, stay tuned as we may introduce this feature in the future!

How do I know what size to choose?

We provide a detailed size chart on each product page. If you’re unsure, we recommend measuring your body dimensions and comparing them with the size guide to choose the best fit.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of purchase. Please contact our customer service team immediately at support@everwear.com for assistance.

I received a damaged or incorrect item. What should I do?

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused. Please contact our customer service team with your order number and photos of the damaged or incorrect item, and we will resolve the issue as quickly as possible by either sending a replacement or issuing a refund.

Do you offer international shipping?

At this time, we only ship within the Philippines. However, we are planning to expand our shipping options internationally in the future, so stay tuned!

How can I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team by emailing us at support@everwear.com. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours.
