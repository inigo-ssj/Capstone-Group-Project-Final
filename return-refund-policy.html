Return & Refund Policy

At Everwear, we want you to love your purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, we're here to help. Below are the details of our return and refund policy.

1. Eligibility for Returns

You can return items within 30 days of delivery if they meet the following conditions:

  • The item must be unused, unworn, and in its original packaging.
  • The item must include all tags, accessories, or promotional items received with it.
  • Proof of purchase or order confirmation must be provided.

Items on clearance or marked as "final sale" are not eligible for returns.

2. Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact us at support@everwear.com with your order number and reason for the return.
  2. We will provide you with a return authorization and shipping instructions.
  3. Pack the item securely and send it to the address provided.

3. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you of the refund approval or rejection. Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days and credited to your original payment method.

Please note that shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (e.g., wrong or defective item sent).

4. Exchanges

If you need a different size or color, we recommend returning the original item and placing a new order.

5. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for returns:

  • Items with signs of use or damage
  • Sale or clearance items

6. Questions?

If you have any questions about our Return & Refund Policy, feel free to contact us at support@everwear.com.
